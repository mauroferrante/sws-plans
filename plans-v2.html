Questions, answered

Most people want to know these before they sign up. If we missed yours, our support team is one click away.

What happens after my 7-day Premium trial ends?
When you register, your account starts with 7 days of full Premium access. No credit card required. On day 8, your account moves to the Free plan automatically. If you'd like to keep Premium or upgrade to Unlimited, you can buy at that point. That's the only time we ask for payment details. Nothing is ever charged without you actively choosing to subscribe.
Do I need a credit card to register?
No, never at signup. We don't ask for payment details when you create your account, regardless of which plan you click. The only time we ask for a card is if you choose to buy a paid plan after your 7-day Premium trial.
Can I cancel anytime, and how?
Yes. Cancel directly from your account settings in two clicks, no email or phone call required. You'll keep your paid features until the end of the current billing period, then move to the Free plan automatically.
What happens to my portfolio data if I downgrade?
Your data stays. If you exceed the Free plan's limits (1 portfolio, 10 holdings), nothing is deleted. You simply can't access data for those additional stocks until you upgrade again.
Can I switch between Premium and Unlimited later?
Yes. Upgrade or downgrade any time from your account. We prorate the difference automatically, so you only pay for what you use.
Is my data and payment info secure?
Payments are processed by Stripe (PCI-DSS Level 1). We never store your card details on our servers. Your account uses TLS 1.3 in transit and AES-256 at rest.
Are there hidden fees?
No. The price you see is the price you pay. VAT or sales tax is shown clearly at checkout where applicable. No setup fees, no per-transaction fees.
